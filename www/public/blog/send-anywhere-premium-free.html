What Send-Anywhere Premium Offers

Send-Anywhere Premium upgrades your account with several features that are limited or unavailable on the free tier. Here is what you get when you pay for premium:

Unlimited File Size

The most significant upgrade is the removal of the 10GB file size limit. Premium users can send files of any size, which is essential for video editors, photographers, developers, and anyone who regularly works with large files.

Reduced Ads

Premium reduces the number of ads you see. Banner ads are minimized, and some interstitial ads are removed. However, premium does not eliminate all ads — you will still encounter some promotional content and sponsored recommendations.

Priority Server Access

Premium users get priority access to Send-Anywhere's servers, which means faster transfers during peak hours when free-tier users experience congestion.

Higher Daily Transfer Limits

Free-tier users have limits on how many transfers they can make per day. Premium increases or removes these limits, allowing for more frequent transfers.

Longer Transfer Code Validity

Transfer codes on the free tier expire after 10 minutes. Premium extends this validity period, giving you more time to share codes with recipients.

Larger Transfer History

Premium provides access to a longer transfer history, making it easier to resend files or track previous transfers.

What You Are Actually Paying For

When you break down the Send-Anywhere Premium subscription, you are paying for:

  • Server storage costs (files are stored on their servers during transfer)
  • Server bandwidth costs (files are routed through their infrastructure)
  • Ad reduction (not elimination — they still show some ads to premium users)
  • Development costs (new features and maintenance)

With 4share's P2P architecture, none of these costs exist. Files transfer directly between devices, so there are no server costs to pass on to users.

The Limits of Send-Anywhere Premium

Even with a premium subscription, Send-Anywhere has significant limitations that many users do not realize until after they have paid.

Still Shows Ads

Premium does not make Send-Anywhere completely ad-free. You will still see some promotional content, sponsored recommendations, and occasional ads. For users who chose premium specifically to escape ads, this is disappointing.

Still Uses Server Relay

Premium gives you priority server access, but it is still server-based. This means:

  • Files must be uploaded to the server first (slow)
  • Then downloaded by the recipient (another slow step)
  • Transfer speed is limited by server capacity and congestion
  • Files exist on third-party servers during transfer (privacy concern)

Even with priority access, server-based transfers are fundamentally slower than P2P transfers.

Still Has Terms of Service Restrictions

Premium accounts are subject to Send-Anywhere's terms of service, which include usage policies, content restrictions, and data handling practices that may not align with your needs.

Subscription Management Hassle

Premium requires an account, which means managing a subscription, handling billing, dealing with payment method updates, and remembering to cancel if you stop using the service.

How to Cancel Send-Anywhere Premium

If you are already paying for Send-Anywhere Premium and want to cancel, here is how to do it on each platform:

Cancel on iPhone (iOS)

  1. Open the Settings app
  2. Tap your name at the top (Apple ID)
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Find Send-Anywhere in the list
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription
  6. Confirm the cancellation

Your premium access will continue until the end of the current billing period.

Cancel on Android

  1. Open the Google Play Store app
  2. Tap your profile icon in the top right
  3. Tap Payments & Subscriptions
  4. Tap Subscriptions
  5. Find Send-Anywhere and tap it
  6. Tap Cancel Subscription
  7. Follow the prompts to confirm

Cancel on Web

  1. Go to Send-Anywhere's website and log in
  2. Navigate to Account Settings
  3. Find the Subscription section
  4. Click Cancel Subscription
  5. Follow the prompts to confirm

Refund Policy

Send-Anywhere may offer refunds for recent charges depending on when you cancel. Contact their support team if you believe you are eligible for a refund. However, many users find that switching to 4share saves enough money that a refund is not necessary to justify the switch.
