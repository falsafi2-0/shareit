WeTransfer, Send-Anywhere, and 4share are three of the most popular file transfer services. But they work very differently under the hood. Here's an exhaustive comparison.

Overview of All Three Services

WeTransfer — A cloud-based file transfer service. Files upload to WeTransfer's servers, then recipients download them. Founded in 2009, now owned by Boost Holding. Offers both free and paid plans.

Send-Anywhere — A file transfer app that uses both cloud relay and direct P2P connections. Files may be stored temporarily on servers. Available as web app and native apps for all platforms.

4share — A pure peer-to-peer file transfer tool. Files transfer directly between browsers using WebRTC. No server storage, no accounts, no limits.

User Experience

WeTransfer: Clean interface, well-known brand. But the free tier is cluttered with ads and the 2GB limit is restrictive.

Send-Anywhere: Functional but ad-heavy. The 6-digit code system is similar to 4share. App download recommended for best experience.

4share: Minimal, fast, no friction. Drag and drop, get code, done. No ads, no pop-ups, no account prompts.

Final Verdict

For most users, 4share is the best option. It's the fastest, most private, and only truly free option. WeTransfer is the most recognized brand but its 2GB free limit is restrictive. Send-Anywhere is a decent middle ground but the ads and server storage are drawbacks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is 4share really free?
Yes, completely free. No premium tier, no ads, no hidden costs. The entire product is free forever.
Why is 4share faster than cloud services?
4share transfers files directly between devices (P2P). Cloud services require uploading to a server first, then downloading from there — doubling the transfer path.
Can I replace WeTransfer with 4share?
Yes. 4share does everything WeTransfer does, but better: no file limits, no ads, faster transfers, and better privacy. The only thing WeTransfer has is brand recognition.
